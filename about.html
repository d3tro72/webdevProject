About Kickoff 2026

Kickoff 2026 is a simple football ticket booking website that allows fans to view international matches and reserve tickets quickly and easily.

Our Mission

To make football ticket booking simple, fast and accessible for every football fan around the world.

Our Vision

To become a trusted platform where supporters can easily discover matches and secure tickets for international football events.
